About

Mallard Web Solutions is local to the Cariboo-Chilcoten region. Founded in Williams Lake, this local business loves to support the local economy and values community connections. Knowing the need for web development in Williams Lake and surrounding area, Mallard Web Solutions was started to give businesses in the area a local option for quality web development and technical consulting.

Education

Mallard Web Solutions was started by Marlie Dueck, a UBC Undergraduate majoring in Computer Science with Honours, born and raised in Williams Lake. She focused her studies on full-stack web development and graduated with numerous awards and recognitions including Graduating Student Award in Computer Science.

Projects

Over the course of her degree, Marlie took on various full-stack web development projects, including an educational video recommender system for students, a registration portal for summer camps, and a medical examination management system. After graduating, she continued to take on projects and expand her repertoire of skills under her employer, implementing projects such as a dashboard for health and safety forms and requirements.

Please feel free to contact for more information about past projects.
